Visit Counter

Thanks for installing the 1self Visit Counter Chrome extension.

By default this extension will log how many times you visit a list of popular websites (see/edit list). You can change which websites you'd like to log visits for at any time.

Visit counter will pick up your visits to selected websites from this point onwards. However, we can also log what you did in the past by looking at your browsing history (again, only for the selected websites).

Visit Counter only logs visits to websites that are in the list. It won't take any notice of any other websites. It also doesn't look at the content, title or long web address of any web pages.
